The Process

How it works

Simple, transparent, and designed for speed.

Step 01

Store your products

Send your inventory to our secure, organized warehouse. We barcode and track every item upon arrival, giving you instant visibility.

Step 02

Orders enter the system

Our system integrates with your sales channels. When an order is placed, it syncs instantly to our dashboard—no manual entry required.

Step 03

Processing & delivery

We pick, pack, and hand off your orders to reliable delivery partners. Quality checks ensure accuracy every time.

Step 04

Real-time visibility

Track order status from "Pending" to "Delivered" in real-time. Your customers get updates, and you stay in control.

Step 05

Fast COD settlement

We collect Cash On Delivery and remit it to you on a reliable schedule, improving your cash flow.

Tech-enabled fulfillment for Lebanese businesses. Clarity, speed, and control in every order.
